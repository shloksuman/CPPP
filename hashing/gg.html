Meet your Quest Partners

Prakhar Gupta and Asim Sayed

Prakhar and Asim's journey together began on a bus trip to Himachal Pradesh, where they met and hit it off.
‚Äç
Prakhar was studying psychology and economics at Columbia University at the time, while Asim was a design consultant at India‚Äôs top design consultancy in Mumbai helping governments and large corporations across the world with digital transformations and product design.

Despite their different backgrounds, they found common ground in their shared passion for learning and sharing knowledge.

Their first project together was the PG Radio podcast with the mission to smuggle culture from the foremost thinkers from all across the world to India.

Asim and Prakhar saw a vision to revolutionise culture, such that the art of self-education is embedded in it forever and for who-ever.

PG Radio quickly gained a devoted following, with over 50,000 listeners tuning in each week to hear interviews with intellectuals, athletes, and artists.

The show became a cultural phenomenon, and was trending in many countries.

Following the success of PG Radio, they embarked on their second adventure with Prakhar Ke Pravachan, a youtube channel focussed on helping people with their life problems in Hindi.

The channel saw massive success, gaining more than 100,000 subscribers within a year with Prakhar. Prakhar a natural conversationalist went on to become a keynote speaker at IITs and other top colleges across the country.

While building PKP, they launched a new project called Learn With Prakhar, to supplement teachings at schools on self-education and communication. Up until Jan 2023, LWP has taught more than 15,000 students with an average rating of 4.8/5 on all of its courses.

Prakhar and Asim's dedication in bringing their vision to life became a beloved resource for students all over India and abroad. All while becoming a profitable business sustaining them to continue on their mission.

In July 2022, they published a book "Don't Shut Up", a handbook on communication for youngsters. Authored by Prakhar himself, the book was sold-out on the pre-launch day itself.

Over the past two years, they have consistently experimented with multiple projects on this mission and successfully delivered to the community.

All of this was possible because of the commitment to their vision, the ability to bring more people to join in on their mission together and the systems they had built to support them in some of the toughest times in their lives.

With extensive research, including studying various studies and speaking with top performers, they now have a better understanding of what the community needs in order to grow and succeed.

They wish to share the knowledge and insights they've gained with this community, and help them reach their full potential.

They bring this systematic process of defining a vision, setting goals, designing a plan of action, to the community all while giving you the support and accountability you need to succeed.
